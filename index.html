What is NDA ?

NDA is a national defence academy which is in kadakwasla,pune. To join this there is an entrance exam which is also known as NDA exam. And then there is a SSB interview. This academy give officers to our country.

How to prepare for this ?

We can prepare for this by books, course, or other material by which you can understand.
